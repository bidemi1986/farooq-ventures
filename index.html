FAQS

Common questions about buying, selling, and investing in LA real estate.

Still have questions about buying or selling in LA? We'd love to help.

What areas of Los Angeles does Farooq Ventures serve?

We specialize in properties across Greater Los Angeles, including Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, West Hollywood, Bel Air, Brentwood, Sherman Oaks, Studio City, Calabasas, and surrounding neighborhoods. Whether you're looking for a coastal retreat, a hillside estate, or a downtown investment property, we know LA's diverse market inside and out.

How do I know if I'm ready to buy a home?

The best first step is a pre-approval from a lender — this confirms your budget and makes your offer competitive. From there, we'll discuss your must-haves, timeline, and neighborhood preferences. We offer a free, no-obligation buyer consultation to walk you through the entire process before you commit to anything.

How long does it take to buy or sell a property in LA?

In LA, most well-priced homes sell within 2–6 weeks of listing. The full close — from accepted offer to keys in hand — typically takes 30 to 45 days in escrow. For buyers, from initial search to closed deal, expect 2–4 months depending on how quickly you find the right property and secure financing.

Do you work with first-time home buyers?

Absolutely. First-time buyers are always welcome and we provide extra guidance at every step — explaining contracts, contingencies, inspections, and closing costs in plain language. We partner with lenders who offer first-time buyer programs and down payment assistance, and we'll make sure you feel confident throughout.

What costs should I budget for when buying a home?

Beyond the purchase price, buyers in California should budget for closing costs (typically 2–3% of the purchase price), a home inspection ($400–$600), appraisal ($500–$800), and title and escrow fees. We provide a detailed cost estimate upfront so there are no surprises at closing.

Can you help me sell my home and buy a new one at the same time?

Yes — we specialize in simultaneous buy-sell transactions. We coordinate both timelines so you're never left without a home or carrying two mortgages. Depending on your equity and market conditions, we can also discuss bridge financing options to make the transition smooth and stress-free.

Do you handle investment and rental properties?

Yes. We assist investors at every level — from first-time rental property buyers to seasoned landlords expanding their portfolio. We analyze cap rates, rental income potential, neighborhood vacancy rates, and long-term appreciation to help you make sound investment decisions. We also connect clients with trusted property management services for hands-off ownership.

What makes Farooq Ventures different from other LA real estate firms?

We are a boutique firm, which means every client works directly with a senior agent — not an assistant or a call center. We combine deep local knowledge, data-driven market analysis, and honest advice to guide you to the right decision, not just the fastest commission. Our clients return to us and refer their friends because we prioritize relationships over transactions.

Farooq Ventures is a premier real estate firm serving buyers, sellers, and investors across Los Angeles. We combine deep local expertise with personalized service to deliver results you can count on.
